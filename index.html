FAQs

Does the other person need an app to read NFC card?

Tap, no app! you can tap your card on the phone that has NFC compatibility, or scan the QR code with their camera.

Which phones are compatible with Near Field Communication Technology?

The majority of new phones has NFC feature installed in their system such as iPhones since 2017, Google phones since 2015, LG since 2014, Samsung since 2014, Huawei since 2016, OnePlus since 2016, HTC since 2015. And for phones that don't support NFC, they can simply scan the QR code.

How many ways to use the card?
